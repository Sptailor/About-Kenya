Kenya is found in east Africa and is bordered by five other countries with the Indian ocean on its East.

The neighbouring countries include :
-Tanzania
-Uganda
-South Sudan
-Ethiopia
-Somalia


Kenya's map reveals a country with a varied topography, ranging from coastal plains to highlands and the Great Rift Valley. Its geographical features have significant implications for climate, ecosystems, and cultural diversity, making Kenya a nation with both natural beauty and cultural richness.
